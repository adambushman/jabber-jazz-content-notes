We’re back

4 years ago started a blog, podcast, YouTube channel to deliver more fan content.

Pandemic hit, shut down the NBA, made the decision to go back to school.

Opted to suspend Jabber Jazz content to focus.

Earned my bachelor’s in data science and statistics.

Joined the team at SLC Dunk.

I decided to resurrect Jabber Jazz.

New look, similar content. Shout out to Lucas Deal for the music!

Expectations:

Media Day Recap

8 hours of live streamed interviews.

4 major themes article

  • Style of play
  • Building relationships
  • Winning culture
  • Opportunities abound

My impressions and thoughts from each statement:

    • Identified issues

    • Visual learner
